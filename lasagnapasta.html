lasagna pasta

Description
dish is a simple lasagna inspired pasta it can be made in only one pot and is much easier to make then traditional pasta. i first learned to make this dish with vegan italian sausage and mushrooms for my brother but now i use regular sausage as it has more flavor.

Ingredients

  • italian sausage
  • garlic clove
  • 24oz can of crushed marzano tomatoes
  • 1 can of tomato paste
  • parmesan cheese
  • mozzarella
  • basil
  • ricotta
  • oven ready lasagna pasta

Steps

  1. brown sausage in pot
  2. add crushed garlic
  3. add crushed tomatoes
  4. add tomato sauce
  5. let simmer
  6. add broken lasagna pasta
  7. cook for about 20 minutes stirring occasionally
  8. when cooked through add mozzarella, mix, then add ricotta
