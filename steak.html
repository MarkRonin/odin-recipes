Steak

Description
This is basically the only way i know how to cook steak, if it ain't broke don't fix it :

Ingredients

  • 1 inch thick steak
  • butter
  • a crushed garlic clove
  • rosemary

Steps

  1. pat a room temp steak dry and thoroughly season with salt and pepper
  2. oil a pan and let it just barely smoke before you add you'r steak
  3. flip after 2 minutes and add a spoon of butter, garlic and rosemary
  4. after the steak reaches an internal temp of 135 take off heat
  5. let rest for 5 minutes and serve with an optional small scoop of butter
