caprese sandwich

Description
i recently made this sandwich at my girlfriends house for lunch and loved it so much we made it again for dinner. i truly think the sun dried tomatoes where the best part.

Ingredients

  • french bread
  • fresh mozzarella
  • sun dried tomatoes in extra virgin olive oil
  • pesto

Steps

  1. cut bread in slices
  2. spread pesto
  3. add slice of mozzarella
  4. add sun dried tomatoes
  5. put in air fryer until bread is toasted and cheese is melted
